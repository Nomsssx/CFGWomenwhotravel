ITINERARY



DAY 1

HAVANA

Say ‘hola’ to Havana today as you arrive at any time. You’ll be met at the airport and transferred to your accommodation in the city. You’ll be staying in a ‘casa particular’, a Cuban style bed and breakfast homestay.
Meals included: Breakfast.

DAY 2 - 3

VINALES

Explore Havana with an included walking tour of the town led by your guide. Spend two days in the beautiful town of Viñales, famous for its tobacco and sugar cane plantations. Included is a day trip to the idyllic coastal spot of Cayo Levisa as well as an informal Spanish lesson with your guide.
Meals: Breakfast and Lunch.

DAY 4

VINALES to PLAYA LARGA

Enjoy a stop off at the beautiful Bay of Pigs, before journeying on to Playa Larga on the south coast of Cuba.
Meals included: Breakfast.

DAY 5 - 6

TRINIDAD

Continue on to Trinidad, enjoy an orientation walk, an inclusive salsa dancing lesson and a home-cooked dinner at your homestay. Optional activities include hiking, horse-riding, beach or valley visits.
Meals included: Lunch and Dinner.


DAY 7

TRINIDAD to HAVANA

We will be stopping off in Santa Clara to visit the famous Ché Guevara monument. Then head back to Havana for a short guided walking tour of this vibrant city.
Meals included: Lunch and Dinner.
