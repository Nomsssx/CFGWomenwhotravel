       EXPLORE BALI (INDONESIA)

    

       
_________________________________________________

       



       

       

       ITINERARY

    

    

    


    

        
    

        
DAY 1

        

        ULUN DANU TEMPLE

        

        
Discover the Ulun Temple which lies at the edge of Baratan Lake in Bedugul in Bali. Situated on the shores of Lake Bratan in the mountains, this beautiful temple is surrounded by the mountain range of the Bedugul region, which provides the temple with a scenic backdrop.
Meals included: Lunch.

        

    


    

    
    

    
DAY 2 - 4

    

    MUCK DIVING IN PADANGBAI

    

    
Situated in the east of Bali, Padang Bai is acclaimed for its diving.  Whatever your diving experience, there are several diving sites to accommodate all. There is also a good chance of seeing sharks, turtles and coral gardens.
Meals included: Breakfast.

    






    
    

    
DAY 5

    

   COOKING CLASS AT BUMBU BALI

    

    
Discover one of the island’s most famous restaurants, Bumbu Bali. Visitors will get the opportunity to learn about local Balinese ingredients and whip up a spectacular traditional Balinese meal.
Meals included: Breakfast.

    






    
    

    
DAY 6 - 9

    

      EXPLORE MENJANGAN ISLAND

    

    
Situated in the north-west of Bali, the island allows visitors to trek, dive and find a plethora of underwater delights such as reefs and colourful fish.
Meals included: Dinner.

    






    
    

    
DAY 10

    

    BALI NIGHTMARKET

    

    
Located near Sanur beach, this market boasts rows of stalls with street food vendors selling anything from traditional Balinese meals to fresh fruits, vegetables and prices. The experience is as local as it gets.
Meals included: Breakfast.
