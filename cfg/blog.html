FIVE INCREDIBLE THINGS TO DO IN EDINBURGH, SCOTLAND

By Nomi Dibidi August 24, 2020

Truly Unforgettable! After visiting Edinburgh for a five of weeks, we can confidently say it is one of the most vibrant cities in the Europe. Edinburgh is a city filled with a little bit of everything. The capital has so much history, bags of culture and incredible places to see. I have noted down some of the ‘must-sees’ when you visit..

Hike up Arthur’s seat

Edinburgh is not only a quant old town filled with cosy pubs! For those who enjoy hiking, spending time outdoors and getting some physical exercise will be delighted to visit the Arthur’s Seat, located in the Holyrood Park. The hike usually lasts around 30 minutes to complete. But trust me it’s worth it when you get to the top. Arthur’s Seat is a great location for the views of Edinburgh.

Hiking Tip: Regardless of what the weather may seem like when you begin your hike, make sure to bring a cardigan or jumper with you just in case. Scottish weather is very unpredictable.

Walk the Water of Leith

If you’re seeking a peaceful escape from the city, you only need to go about a mile from the Royal Mile to find the Water of Leith Walkwayi. This is a 12-mile route running alongside the waterside with statues, bridges and lots of greenery along the way. The full route covers a long stretch filled with captivating views in Stockbridge and Dean Village.

Edinburgh's Secret Garden

Dr Neil’s Gardenb is one of Scotland’s most remarkable gardens. Finding Edinburgh’s secret garden is something of an adventure in itself. Nestled away behind a 12th century church in the picturesque village of Duddingston.(More text).

6 REASONS WHY MALAYSIA WILL TRIGGER YOUR WANDERLUST

By Sarah Acheampong May 21, 2020

As they say, Malaysia is Truly Asia. This is one country where you can set foot and get to experience the best of what Asia has on offer. A multicultural country, Malaysia gives you a glimpse of the heritage of Chinese, Indians, Sri Lanka and other Asian countries as well. In terms of landscape, this country allures with beaches, hills and even glistening cityscapes like that of Kuala Lumpur. It has its arms open wide to greet travellers from across the world, serving them delicious food, pampering them with a buzzing shopping scenario and traditional spas. I recently returned home from my trip to Malaysia and I am looking forward to booking my ticket back!

1. Luxury on a budget

Whether you’re on a shoestring budget or are looking for a splurging vacation, Malaysia will please you! You can choose to stay in a welcoming hostel or pick a luxury hotel with all the modern amenities. Most of the tourist attractions and sites are very reasonably priced so you wouldn't have to worry about going bankrupt. Malaysia provides you with the ability to explore the depths of it's landscape whilst also saving a lot of money. I still have a few ringgit's (malaysian currency) left ready to exchange back!

2. Stunning Beach Getaways

You haven’t truly visited Malaysia until you have seen the beauty that Langkawi is. There are a variety of archipelagos here in Malaysia, which honestly will put even the Bahamas to shame. You will agree too! .

3. Rainforests

I don’t think I need to continue listing reasons to visit Malaysia once we realise how affluent Malaysia is in terms of nature and wildlife as well. Almost two-third of this country is covered in rainforests, serving as a habitat to twenty percent of world’s animal species.

Best places in Malaysia for nature and wildlife: Taman Negara National Park, Kinabalu National Park, and Gunung Mulu National Park

4. Myriad of adventure and activities

This country understands that travel is not limited to sightseeing, so it spoils you with choices of activities. You could be a beginner or an expert, there still be a long list. Ranging from sky diving and scuba diving to witnessing the enchantment of marine life, the choice is yours.


5. Food Coma

It’s said if you don’t let yourself indulge in the street food of Malaysia, your trip isn’t really complete. Here, you get to titillate your taste buds with a delicious blend of flavours and spices. This is where you get to relish the best of noodles, rice, soup and what not!


6. Favorable Weather

The country experiences favorable weather conditions almost all around the year. Thanks to its huge size, you can always pick the best for yourself regardless of when you are traveling. As most part of the country is covered in rainforest, you will indeed enjoy pleasant weather.

12 STRAIGHT-TO-THE-POINT REASONS WHY YOU MUST VISIT AUSTRALIA

By Anastasia Sviridova March 7, 2020

Once you’ll be in, you’ll never want to leave! There’s no doubt Australia is one of the most remote, fascinating, and highly developed countries in the world. With so many good reasons to visit Australia, like its diverse landscapes, friendly locals and a long list of amazing ‘bucket list’ experiences, it’s clear why so many backpackers head here first including MYSELF!

Endowed with a myriad of natural wonders, plenty of wildlife, dazzling beaches, cosmopolitan metropolises, and large expanses of the outback, it’s easy to see why this captivating country is such a complete travel destination. It not only matches every taste, budget, age, or interest out there, but it also delivers some unique experiences almost impossible to find somewhere else. Australia is so much more than just a paradise for adventure seekers, it is a delight for foodies, art lovers, and budget travellers.

Lets get into it!

1. The very existence of Goon ensures that you can drink every single day and still keep within your budget
2. There are 11,000 beaches to choose from!
3. You have over 500 National Parks in Australia to explore
4. You can Snorkel or Scuba Dive in the world's largest living organism - the Great Barrier Reef
5. And snorkel alongside the gigantic Whale Sharks in Ningaloo Reef
6. …Or the playful Sea Lions in Port Lincoln
7. It is home to Whitehaven Beach in the Whitsunday Islands, which is continuously voted in the Top 10 World's Best Beaches lists 8. You can climb the highest rock in the world in Western Australia - Mt Augustus
9. You can eat Avocados every day, with almost everything and not get frowned upon
10. You can drink everyday of the week, at anytime of the day and not get frowned upon
11. You can eat the country's national emblems and not get frowned upon - the Emu and Kangaroo!
12. You can find $4.95 Dominos pizza in most parts of Australia
