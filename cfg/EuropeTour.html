ULTIMATE EUROPE TRIP

ITINERARY



DAY 1

LONDON to PARIS, FRANCE

Travel from London to Paris via a ferry across the English Channel. France is known the world over for its romance, but there might be no other region that encompasses its quintessential beauty—the cities, villages, hills, and beaches—quite like Provence. In the evening enjoy a guided driving tour of Paris.
Meals included: Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 2

PARIS

Spend the day exploring Paris. Optional bike tour. Optional Seine River cruise where you'll be able to see the best of Paris, the city of love. Or visit a cabaret show where there is art the synchronization, the wild costumes and, bien sûr, the inter-act performers who do interesting things with animals and ventriloquist dummies (though rarely at the same time).
Meals included: Breakfast.

DAY 3

PARIS to SWISS ALPS, SWITZERLAND

Stay in the Alps at the foot of the Jungfraujoch Mountain. The Jungfraujoch is an alpine attraction and it is located in between the well known mountains Mönch and Jungfrau.
Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 4

SWISS ALPS

Hike or rail to the summit of Jungfrau Mountain; home of Europe’s highest rail station. When it comes to hiking in the Jungfrau region, you can't go wrong. Wherever nature lovers turn, they will find perfectly signposted routes: to summits or mountain lakes, into valleys or along streams. Whether pleasure or ambition is the motivation: if you follow the yellow arrows, you will find your way.
Meals included:Dinner.

DAY 5

SWISS ALPS to VENICE, ITALY

Drive into Italy to your resort, across the lagoon from Venice. You are guaranteed to be met by adrenaline-inducing hairpin turns, steep inclines and picture perfect views.
Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 6

VENICE

We will be taking a vaporetto ride on the Grand Canal during the day to discover more of Venice. Optional gondola ride – a must-do!
Meals included: Breakfast.

DAY 7

VENICE TO MUNICH, GERMANY

Visit Germany’s “Toy Town”, Munich for a guided tour and time in the beer halls. We will spend time in the Hofbrauhaus, the world's most famous beer hall.
Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 8

HEIDELBERG

Visit the picturesque West German town of Heidelberg filled with fairytale 18th century architecture and cobblestone streets.
Meals included: Lunch.

DAY 9

HEIDELBERG to AMSTERDAM

Visit a Dutch farm en route to Amsterdam, where you can enjoy free time around the city and the infamous red light district!
Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 10

AMSTERDAM

Free time to discover the Dutch city. Well known for its canals, beautiful houses and coffee shops, there's plenty to do in this bustling city. Farewell dinner river cruise.
Meals included: Dinner.
