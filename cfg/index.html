THE COMPANY

  
Key features of our company

  

    

      
      
Priority

      
We aim to build good relationships with our customers. We want to promote a positive, helpful and friendly environment to ensure you leave us with a great impression. Customers are a priority

    

    

      
      
Passion

      
We are absolutely passionate about promoting confidence and encouraging our customers to take a leap of faith to spend some time in soliude to embark on an inward journey of self-discovery

    

    

      
      
Purpose

      
When Women Travelling the World first opened, the idea was simple: creating a blog to show and encourage solo-travelling. 2 years later, we brought features from the realm of self-discovering travelling and developed into a travel network agency.

    

    

      
      
Support

      
We stand as women in unity! we believe there is so much power when we come together and empower each other to defeat and reverse societal stereotypes! We are committed to changing the lives of others.
