ITINERARY



DAY 1

DUNROBIN CASTLE & GARDENS

Dunrobin Castle & Gardens are beautifully situated with the castle overlooking the ornately designed gardens. A bird of prey display is given at certain times of the day and is well worth watching.
Meals: Breakfast and Lunch.

DAY 2

CITY OF EDINBURGH TOUR

Edinburgh is a bustling city filled with charm and a little bit of everything. Starting off with Edinburgh Castle which dominates the city’s skyline, we will explore the historic building which dates back to the 7th century. We will then continue the day by discovering the story of Scotland at the Museum of Scotland. Then finish the day off inside the city’s most celebrated Michelin-starred restaurant, ‘The Kitchin’.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 3

ISLE OF SKYE

The Island of Skye is renowned for its natural beauty, history and wildlife. We will be exploring the Island including taking the Stardust boat trip from Portree to see the sea eagles. Then we will finish the day off by watching the sunset over the Minch.
Meals included: Breakfast.

DAY 4

ST. ANDREWS

Packed with history, tradition and scenic views – St Andrews is a real Scottish gem. This small city is set on the coast of Fife to the northeast of Edinburgh. We will be starting the day with brunch at Mitchells, a restaurant and deli specialising in local produce. Then we will be taking a walk around the ruins of St Andrews Castle, perched on a clifftop overlooking the sea. In the evening we will walk back to the waterfront for dinner at the Seafood Ristorante.
Meals included: Brunch and Dinner.
