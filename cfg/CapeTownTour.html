CAPE TOWN, SOUTH AFRICA


_________________________________________________



ITINERARY



DAY 1

HIKE LION'S HEAD

Looming above Cape Town and the surrounding ocean like a sentinel, Lion’s Head is an iconic piece of the city’s wondrous topography. At 669 metres above sea level, its peak offers panoramic views of the city, Table Bay and the Atlantic Ocean, making it a wildly popular destination.
Meals included: Breakfast and Dinner.

DAY 2

SEGWAY THROUGH THE STELLENBOSCH VINEYARDS

Glide through rows of vines at Spier Wine Farm on a two-wheeled PT (personal transporter), and relish the surrounding mountain vistas, while also learning some interesting facts about Spier’s biodynamic farming techniques.
Meals included: Lunch.

DAY 3

TANDEM-PARAGLIDE OVER THE ATLANTIC OCEAN

Experience Cape Town’s magnificent topography from up high – and get your adrenaline pumping – with an experienced instructor. Flights last between five and 30 minutes, depending on the wind. In perfect weather conditions, the instructor will show you how to steer so that you can fly the paraglider yourself!
Meals included: Dinner.

DAY 4

HOT-AIR BALLON IN THE WINELANDS

Trains, trams and automobiles… there are many ways to experience the Cape Winelands, but few that allow you to soar over them like a bird. Lift-off is at sunrise, but the early start is definitely worth it for the views you’ll be treated to as you float over the rolling vineyards and lush meadows that the Cape Winelands are so famous for.
Meals included: Dinner.

DAY 5 - 6

A TUK-TUK WINE-TASTING TOUR OF FRANSCHHOEK

We will experience an awayday to Franschhoek. You’ll be as delighted as we were to discover this easy, convenient (and affordable) way to get around the valley – via Tuk-Tuk. Because, let’s be frank, nobody wants to get behind the wheel when there are wine farms to visit, nectars to sample, gourmet restaurants to eat at, and picnics to be devoured.
Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 7

DRINKS AT AN INNERCITY ROOFTOP BAR

Slotted atop ever-popular Kloof Street, is Up Yours, the rooftop terrace above Yours Truly. The humming atmosphere is set alight by twinkling fairy lights and hanging plants. Super-cool local and international DJs and live acoustic sessions take things to the next level (pun intended).
Meals included: Dinner.
